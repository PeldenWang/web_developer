Best Foods in Bhutan

Top 3 food must try recommend by azha

Ema Datshi with rice

It is spicy Bhutanese stew made from hot chilli peppers and cheese. It is among the most famous dishes in Bhutan.

Kewa Datshi

A flavorful vegetarian stew made with potato, cheese, chilis, and garlic. Try this traditional Bhutanese dish for a taste of authentic cuisine.

Shamu Datshi

In pan, place the sliced mushrooms, water, butter, and salt over high heat and bring to boil. Reduce the heat to medium-low, cover, and cook 5 mins till the mushrooms are cooked.
